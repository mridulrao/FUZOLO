Box Cricket

We all enjoy and love a good game of cricket. But, it's very hard to orchestrate a full-fledged cricket match involving 22 players (11 in each team), a well-maintained pitch, a big playground or arena, and a heavy bunch of safety gear. All of this is not easily acquired. That's why, we provide Box Cricket! Box Cricket is an exciting spin to your traditional cricket game. Introducing a paradigm shift to conventional cricket, this concept approaches the game from an entirely different direction, which further persuades our players to put their most intense efforts forward. It shares all the basic rules and regulations of cricket, but the matches are shorter, more fast-paced, and enhanced for higher engagement and excitement. How is it played? Box cricket is played within an enclosed area over a turf pitch. Each match involves 2 teams consisting of 4-7 players each. Our All-Weather Pitches combined with Flood Lights comprise of the ideal set up for late night games, as well. During your time, you can play casually with your friends or use the pitches to get extra training ahead of your next match. We can't wait to see you on the ground!
